About

This started with an argument between friends on which team is better midseason. Myself being a Miami Heat fan and my friend, being a fan of the Minnesota Timberwolves found that finding data about individual players is easy, but stats about teams compared to other teams can be hard to find. So I built this platform to solve the question: "How many times has one team played another team and which team has more wins."
